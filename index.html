Our Story

A Family Legacy of Royal Flavors

మా ఇంటి వంట సంప్రదాయం, మీ శుభకార్యానికి ప్రత్యేక విందు

Sri Tulasi Caterers is a proud family-run business rooted in generations of culinary tradition. What began in small village hotels has grown into a modern catering and cloud kitchen service, while staying true to our authentic Telangana flavors.

Our journey is built on home-ground masalas and treasured family recipes passed down through our elders. Cooking has always been a part of our family’s identity, shaped by the dedication of our fathers and grandfathers.

Carrying this legacy forward, Mr. Srinivas followed his passion for food, inspired by his family’s deep connection to the industry. With the constant support and strength of Mrs. Tulasi, together they have nurtured and grown Sri Tulasi Caterers into what it is today.

Blending tradition with modern techniques, we are committed to delivering quality, authenticity, and memorable experiences in every dish we serve.
